About

I am a CS PhD student at Stanford University specializing in Theoretical RL and Wearable biomedical sensors. I am excited about the potential of cheap wearable biomedical sensors such as the Oura ring, CGM devices, and EEG helmets. In particular, we develop theoretical guarantees on temporal dependent data and software tools to work with Wearable devices website. Previously, I have constructed machine learning algorithms for a wide range of topics, including numerical tasks, NLP, bioinformatics, and medtech.
